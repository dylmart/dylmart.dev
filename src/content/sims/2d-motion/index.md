Originally written in Web VPython for Physics161. Type your own v₀x, v₀y, and aᵧ, or drag from the launch point (the marker at the origin, not the flying ball) to aim a new shot by feel. Acceleration defaults to gravity, so the ball falls back to the ground and stops right where it lands. The dotted line is the predicted path for the current launch parameters.
